My Shelf

My Shelf is an app where you can exchange books that you have already read with books that you wish to read based on your interest or location.

How does it work?

If you have a book that you already read and wanted to exchange it with friends or people who live near by, you can post your book and specify what you want in return.

When you post a book you can choose if you are giving the book away for free, exchange it with your wish list or specify your request.

Broden Your Circle

To Increase chances of getting books that you like to read, you can search other users based on thier interest, distance from your location and add them to your circle.

You will get updates from other users that you are following and you can send them direct message from the app.
